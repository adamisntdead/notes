### Réamhfhocal - Where Urús Appear

* ag an - at the
* ar an - on the
* as an - out of the 
* faoin - under the 
* ón - from the
* roimh an - before the 
* leis an - with the
* tríd an - through the

**Exceptions**: 'dntls', ná cuir urú leo seachas nuair a bhíonn an 's' ainmfhocal bainincineach m.s. leis an tseanbhean tógann 's' 't'
