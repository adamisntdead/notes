# Numbers

You will need to be very clear with the numbers, especially for things like your exam number

## Phrases

- Counting: **ag comhareamh**
- 0: **a náid**
- 1: **a haon**
- 2: **a dó**
- 3: **a trí**
- 4: **a cuig**
- 5: **a cúig**
- 6: **a sé**
- 7: **a seacht**
- 8: **a hocht**
- 9: **a naoi**
